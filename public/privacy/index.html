Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how 21 Events Oy ("we", "us") collects, uses and protects personal data when you visit miningsummit.eu or register for, attend or otherwise interact with the European Mining Summit ("EMS").

1. What data we collect

We collect personal data you provide directly and a limited amount of technical data generated when you visit our website.

Data you provide

  • Ticket purchase — name, email address, billing details, company (if provided). Collected by our ticketing partner Tito on our behalf.
  • Sponsorship inquiry — name, business email, company, sponsorship tier of interest, message. Submitted via the sponsor form on miningsummit.eu.
  • Email correspondence — anything you send us at info@miningsummit.eu or other channels.

Data collected automatically

  • Server logs — IP address, user agent, requested URL, timestamp. Retained briefly by our hosting provider for security and abuse prevention.
  • Cookies — only strictly necessary cookies set by the ticketing widget when you initiate a purchase. We do not run advertising, analytics or tracking cookies.

2. Why we use it and on what legal basis

  • Performing the ticket contract — to issue your ticket, send confirmation and event-critical updates (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).
  • Legitimate interest — to respond to sponsorship inquiries, maintain the website, prevent abuse, and contact you about EMS-related matters you initiated (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • Legal obligation — to retain accounting records and invoices as required by Finnish law (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR).
  • Consent — for any optional marketing communications, only after you have opted in (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR). You can withdraw consent at any time.

3. Who processes your data

We use the following processors. Each is bound by a data processing agreement or equivalent commitment.

  • Tito (Ireland) — ticket sales and attendee management.
  • Formspree (United States) — sponsor inquiry form delivery. Transfers to the US rely on the EU–US Data Privacy Framework or standard contractual clauses.
  • Cloudflare (United States, EU edge) — hosting and content delivery for miningsummit.eu.
  • Google Fonts (United States) — webfont delivery; processes your IP address when fonts load.
  • Email and office tooling used by the organizer to manage correspondence.

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share data with third parties only as needed to operate the event (e.g. venue access lists, name badges) or where required by law.

4. How long we keep your data

  • Ticket and accounting records — retained for the period required by Finnish accounting law (currently 6 years from end of the relevant accounting period).
  • Sponsorship and other correspondence — retained while there is an active relationship and for a reasonable period afterwards, normally no longer than 3 years from last contact.
  • Server logs — short-term only, typically no more than 30 days.

5. Your rights

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Have inaccurate data corrected.
  • Have your data erased where applicable.
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Receive your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw any consent you have given.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman (tietosuoja.fi).

To exercise any of these rights, email info@miningsummit.eu. We will respond within one month.

6. Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, alteration or disclosure. All data transmission to and from miningsummit.eu is encrypted via HTTPS.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated on this page with an updated "last updated" date.
